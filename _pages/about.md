I'm Feng Sheng, an undergraduate student at the [Department of Automation, Tsinghua University](https://www.au.tsinghua.edu.cn/), enrolled in the AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) training program.

I really like solving real-world problems with mathematical modeling methods and computer programs. I am also fond of the human recognition process and its relation with artificial intelligence.

## Research Interests

- **Machine Learning** — the ideas behind various frameworks
- **Mathematical Modeling** — the bridge between reality and virtual environments
- **Embodied Intelligence** — interaction with humans and other AI agents
- **Philosophy of AI** — what is "value" and how do we define it?

## About Me

I'm always eager to learn new things, from mathematics to programming, from physics to English. I excel at scheduling my self-learning process, controlling the pace to maintain interest and enthusiasm. I thrive in team environments, mapping general plans and exchanging ideas with teammates.

Beyond academics, I enjoy playing basketball, reading philosophy, and exploring the cognitive processes behind human decision-making. These diverse interests enrich my perspective and inspire my research.

My long-term goal is to contribute to the development of beneficial AGI that serves humanity.
